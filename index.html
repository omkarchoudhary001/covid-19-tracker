How Virus Spread

How Contagion Coronavirus

Coronavirus primarily spreads through respiratory droplets when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or talks. It can also spread by touching contaminated surfaces and then touching the face.

  • Human Contact

    Direct interaction with an infected person facilitates transmission of the coronavirus.

  • Air Transmission

    Coronavirus can spread through the air via respiratory droplets released when infected individuals cough, sneeze, or talk.

  • Contaminated Objects

    Touching surfaces or objects contaminated with the virus and then touching the face can lead to transmission of coronavirus.

Corona Virus Symptoms

Coronavirus symptoms vary but commonly include fever, cough, shortness of breath, fatigue, body aches, loss of taste or smell, sore throat, and headache.

  • Coughing And Sneezing

    Common symptoms of coronavirus include coughing and sneezing, which can spread the virus to others.

  • Hot Fever

    Coronavirus infection often presents with a high fever, indicating an immune response to the virus.

  • Strong Headacke

    Severe headaches can occur as a symptom of coronavirus infection, often accompanied by other flu-like symptoms.

  • Shortness Of Breath

    Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath is a serious symptom of coronavirus and may require immediate medical attention.

  • Confusion

    Coronavirus can cause neurological symptoms such as confusion or difficulty concentrating, especially in severe cases.

  • Sore Throat

    Sore throat is a common symptom of coronavirus infection, along with other respiratory symptoms like cough and congestion.

Prevention Coronavirus

Preventing coronavirus involves practicing good hygiene like frequent handwashing, wearing masks in public, maintaining physical distance, avoiding large gatherings, and staying updated with reliable health information.

  • Wash Your Hands For 20sec

    Prevent the spread of germs by covering your nose and mouth with a tissue or your elbow when sneezing or coughing

  • Cover Nose and Mouth When Sneezing

    Help reduce the transmission of coronavirus by wearing a mask in public settings where social distancing is challenging.

  • Wear A Mask If Available

    Use a tissue or your elbow to cover your nose and mouth when sneezing to prevent the spread of respiratory droplets. Wear A Mask If Available: Wear a mask in crowded or enclosed spaces to protect yourself and others from coronavirus transmission.
